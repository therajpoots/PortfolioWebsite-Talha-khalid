About Rana Talha Khalid - Aspiring Biomedical Researcher

Rana Talha Khalid is a passionate Biomedical Engineering graduate from Riphah International University, Lahore, Pakistan, dedicated to advancing Smart Biomaterials, AI-driven Biosensors, and Wearable Technologies for early disease detection and personalized healthcare management. Proficient in COMSOL Multiphysics (material and biosensor simulation), MATLAB, LabVIEW, and SolidWorks for modeling and design. Experienced in circuit simulation using Proteus, Multisim, and EasyEDA, and in microcontroller programming with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and PIC. Skilled in AI model development using TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Python, focusing on biosignal processing, deep learning (CNN, LSTM, Transformers), and device optimization. Adept at Flutter and Dart for mobile health app development, and knowledgeable in thermal and material degradation analysis, 3D prototyping, and data-driven healthcare system design.
